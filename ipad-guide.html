    IPAD TRAINING GUIDE FOR SENIORS

    A picture-illustrated guide to using the iPad for new learners

    OVERVIEW

    The Getting Started iPad Training guide was developed to help older adults without experience using technology learn foundational skills. During pilot training sessions, many new learners requested printed instructions to help reinforce key concepts.

    I led the development and design of the guide. Through a series of interviews and user testing, we identified five categories of tablet use that were most important and designed a series of step-by-step lessons for key features.
